BIRDEYE API KEY
Cipher uses Birdeye for Trending and Top Volume scans. Your key is stored locally in your browser and never sent anywhere except Birdeye's API.
1
Go to birdeye.so/data-api and click Get API Key or Start for Free
2
Sign up with your email address. No credit card required for the free Standard tier.
3
Once logged in, go to your Dashboard. Your API key will be displayed there — copy it.
4
Paste your key below and click Save. The Standard (free) tier gives you 30,000 compute units/month — plenty for manual scanning.
⚠ Your key is saved in localStorage — local to this browser only. If you clear browser data you'll need to re-enter it. Never share your API key with anyone.
